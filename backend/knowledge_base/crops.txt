CROP: RICE
Season: Kharif (June-November)
Soil: Clay or clay loam, pH 5.5-7.0
NPK Requirements: Nitrogen 120kg/ha, Phosphorus 60kg/ha, Potassium 40kg/ha
Irrigation: Requires flooded conditions, 1200-2000mm water
Sowing: Transplant seedlings 25-30 days old, spacing 20x15cm
Growth Duration: 110-150 days
Pest Control: Watch for stem borer, brown planthopper, blast disease
Fertilizer Schedule: Apply half N + full P + full K at transplanting, remaining N in two splits at tillering and panicle initiation
Harvest: When 80% grains are golden yellow, moisture content around 20-25%
Yield: 4-6 tonnes per hectare
Special Notes: Maintain 5cm standing water during vegetative stage

CROP: MAIZE
Season: Kharif and Rabi
Soil: Well-drained loamy soil, pH 6.0-7.5
NPK Requirements: Nitrogen 120kg/ha, Phosphorus 60kg/ha, Potassium 40kg/ha
Irrigation: Critical at knee-high, tasseling and grain filling stages
Sowing: Seed rate 20kg/ha, spacing 60x20cm, depth 3-5cm
Growth Duration: 90-120 days
Pest Control: Fall armyworm, stem borer, turcicum leaf blight
Fertilizer Schedule: One third N at sowing, one third at knee high, one third at tasseling, full P and K at sowing
Harvest: When husks turn brown and dry, grain moisture 20-25%
Yield: 5-8 tonnes per hectare
Special Notes: Cannot tolerate waterlogging, ensure good drainage

CROP: CHICKPEA
Season: Rabi (October-March)
Soil: Sandy loam to clay loam, pH 6.0-8.0, well drained
NPK Requirements: Nitrogen 20kg/ha, Phosphorus 40kg/ha, Potassium 20kg/ha
Irrigation: One irrigation at pre-flowering and pod filling stage
Sowing: Seed rate 60-80kg/ha, spacing 30x10cm
Growth Duration: 90-120 days
Pest Control: Pod borer Helicoverpa is the major pest, wilt disease
Fertilizer Schedule: Full dose at sowing, being legume needs less nitrogen
Harvest: When 70% pods turn brown and leaves shed
Yield: 1.5-2.5 tonnes per hectare
Special Notes: Rhizobium inoculation of seeds increases yield by 15-20%

Irrigation: Drought tolerant but needs moisture at flowering
Sowing: Seed rate 12-15kg/ha, spacing 60-75x30cm

CROP: POMEGRANATE
Season: Perennial fruit crop
Soil: Well drained loamy soil, pH 5.5-7.0, tolerates slightly alkaline
NPK Requirements: Nitrogen 625g/plant/year, Phosphorus 250g/plant/year, Potassium 250g/plant/year
Irrigation: Drip irrigation preferred, 8-10 liters per plant per day
Sowing: Propagated through cuttings, spacing 5x4m or 4x3m for high density
Growth Duration: Starts bearing in 2-3 years, economic life 25-30 years
Pest Control: Anar butterfly, bark eating caterpillar, bacterial blight
Fertilizer Schedule: Split into 3 doses, before flowering, after fruit set, and before harvest
Harvest: Fruits ready 5-7 months after flowering, metallic sound on tapping indicates maturity
Yield: 20-25 tonnes per hectare
Special Notes: Withhold irrigation for 1-2 months to induce flowering

CROP: BANANA
Season: Perennial, planted year round
Soil: Rich loamy well drained soil, pH 6.0-7.5
NPK Requirements: Nitrogen 200g/plant, Phosphorus 60g/plant, Potassium 300g/plant
Irrigation: Every 3-4 days, drip irrigation most efficient
Sowing: Planted from suckers or tissue culture plants, spacing 2x2m or 1.8x1.8m
Growth Duration: 11-15 months from planting to harvest
Pest Control: Banana weevil, nematodes, Fusarium wilt, Sigatoka leaf spot
Fertilizer Schedule: Every 2 months in 6 equal splits
Harvest: Harvest when fingers are full and rounded, 75-80% maturity for distant markets
Yield: 30-40 tonnes per hectare
Special Notes: Support plants with stakes before bunch emergence

CROP: MANGO
Season: Perennial, flowers January-March, harvest May-July
Soil: Deep well drained alluvial or loamy soil, pH 5.5-7.5
NPK Requirements: Nitrogen 500g/tree/year for mature tree, increases with age
Irrigation: Critical at flowering and fruit development, avoid during flowering
Sowing: Grafted plants preferred, spacing 10x10m standard or 5x5m for high density
Growth Duration: Grafted trees bear in 3-5 years
Pest Control: Mango hopper, fruit fly, powdery mildew, anthracnose
Fertilizer Schedule: Once before monsoon and once after harvest
Harvest: When fruit skin changes color and floats in water when mature
Yield: 10-15 tonnes per hectare for mature orchard
Special Notes: Biennial bearing is common, manage with proper pruning

CROP: GRAPES
Season: Perennial, pruned twice a year in most regions
Soil: Well drained sandy loam to loam, pH 6.5-7.5
NPK Requirements: Nitrogen 45kg/ha, Phosphorus 45kg/ha, Potassium 45kg/ha per year
Irrigation: Drip irrigation, withhold 4-6 weeks before harvest for sugar accumulation
Sowing: Planted from cuttings, spacing 3x1.5m or 2.7x0.9m
Growth Duration: Productive from 3rd year, economic life 25-30 years
Pest Control: Downy mildew, powdery mildew, thrips, mealybug
Fertilizer Schedule: Split into pre-pruning, post-pruning, berry formation stages
Harvest: Grape clusters when sugar content reaches 20-22 brix
Yield: 20-30 tonnes per hectare
Special Notes: Training on bower or trellis system essential

CROP: APPLE
Season: Perennial, flowers March-April, harvest August-October
Soil: Well drained loamy soil, pH 5.5-6.5, needs chilling requirement
NPK Requirements: Nitrogen 70g/tree young increasing to 700g mature
Irrigation: Critical at pink bud, petal fall, and fruit development
Sowing: Grafted plants on rootstock, spacing 6x6m standard or 3x1.5m high density
Growth Duration: Grafted trees bear in 3-5 years
Pest Control: Codling moth, wooly aphid, scab, fire blight
Fertilizer Schedule: Before bud break in spring, after harvest in fall
Harvest: 140-150 days after full bloom, starch iodine test confirms maturity
Yield: 10-20 tonnes per hectare depending on variety and age
Special Notes: Requires 1000-1600 chilling hours below 7 degrees Celsius

CROP: PAPAYA
Season: Planted year round, bears within 10-12 months
Soil: Well drained sandy loam, pH 6.0-7.0, cannot tolerate waterlogging
NPK Requirements: Nitrogen 200g/plant/year, Phosphorus 200g/plant/year, Potassium 250g/plant/year
Irrigation: Every 7-10 days, avoid wetting crown and stem
Sowing: Raised nursery beds, transplant at 45 days, spacing 2x2m
Growth Duration: First harvest 9-12 months after transplanting
Pest Control: Papaya mealybug, papaya ringspot virus through aphids
Fertilizer Schedule: Every 2 months in equal splits
Harvest: When fruit shows yellow streaks, for papain extraction harvest green
Yield: 40-50 tonnes per hectare per year
Special Notes: Plant 10% male plants to ensure pollination

CROP: COCONUT
Season: Perennial, year round production
Soil: Sandy loam to red laterite, pH 5.2-8.0, tolerates salinity
NPK Requirements: Nitrogen 500g/palm/year, Phosphorus 320g/palm/year, Potassium 1200g/palm/year
Irrigation: 40-45 liters per palm every alternate day in summer
Sowing: Seed nuts planted in pits, spacing 7.5x7.5m triangular
Growth Duration: Starts bearing in 6-8 years, economic life 60-80 years
Pest Control: Rhinoceros beetle, red palm weevil, coconut mite, root wilt disease
Fertilizer Schedule: Two equal splits, May-June and September-October
Harvest: Nuts harvested every 45 days, at 12 months maturity for copra
Yield: 80-100 nuts per palm per year
Special Notes: Potassium is the most critical nutrient for coconut

CROP: COTTON
Season: Kharif (May-June to November-December)
Soil: Black cotton soil or deep alluvial, pH 7.0-8.0
NPK Requirements: Nitrogen 120kg/ha, Phosphorus 60kg/ha, Potassium 60kg/ha
Irrigation: At square formation, flowering, and boll development
Sowing: Seed rate 2.5kg/ha for Bt cotton, spacing 90x60cm
Growth Duration: 160-180 days
Pest Control: Bollworm, whitefly, aphids, bacterial blight
Fertilizer Schedule: One third N at sowing, one third at 45 days, one third at 75 days
Harvest: Pick bolls when fully open, 4-5 pickings over the season
Yield: 2-3 tonnes of seed cotton per hectare
Special Notes: Bt cotton has resistance to bollworm but whitefly management still needed

CROP: JUTE
Season: Kharif (March-July)
Soil: Well drained sandy loam to clay loam, pH 6.0-7.5
NPK Requirements: Nitrogen 60kg/ha, Phosphorus 30kg/ha, Potassium 30kg/ha
Irrigation: Requires good rainfall, 1000-2000mm, supplemental at early stage
Sowing: Seed rate 7-8kg/ha, broadcast sowing, thin to 4-6cm spacing
Growth Duration: 100-120 days
Pest Control: Jute hairy caterpillar, stem rot, soft rot disease
Fertilizer Schedule: Half N at sowing, remaining at 30 days after sowing
Harvest: At 50% flowering stage for best fibre quality
Yield: 2.0-2.5 tonnes of dry fibre per hectare
Special Notes: Ret in clean water for 10-15 days for fibre extraction

CROP: COFFEE
Season: Perennial, flowers after dry spell, harvest November-February
Soil: Well drained deep loam to clay loam, pH 6.0-6.5, shade loving
NPK Requirements: Nitrogen 40g/plant increasing to 200g mature, similar P and K
Irrigation: Sprinkler irrigation, critical at flowering and bean development
Sowing: Grown from seeds in nursery, transplanted at 6-9 months, spacing 3x3m
Growth Duration: Starts bearing in 3-4 years
Pest Control: White stem borer, shot hole borer, leaf rust, black rot
Fertilizer Schedule: Three splits, pre-monsoon, mid-monsoon, post-monsoon
Harvest: Hand pick only red ripe cherries, selective picking improves quality
Yield: 700-900kg of clean coffee per hectare
Special Notes: Shade trees like silver oak are essential for quality coffee
